The Framework

Six Realms of Competence

You can't fake true confidence. It has to be earned across every dimension of life. Audit yourself honestly — then get to work.

⚡

Realm 01

Skillfully Competent

Do you have valuable skills the market actually pays for? Skills are only meaningful if they generate money, opportunities, or leverage.

Pick one skill and go deep for 6 months
Sales, copywriting, coding, public speaking
Measure by what it produces — not hours put in
🔥

Realm 02

Physically Competent

Your body is a reflection of your habits. Take care of it and your mental clarity, confidence, and energy all go up — automatically.

Train 4x per week minimum
Sleep 7–9 hrs, eat for energy not comfort
Look like someone who has their life together
🧠

Realm 03

Mentally Competent

Can you handle pressure without breaking? The mentally strong adapt — they don't vent. Resilience is a skill, not a personality trait.

When hit hard — adapt, don't complain
Sit with discomfort deliberately every day
Build a bias toward action over analysis
🤝

Realm 04

Socially Competent

Your network is your net worth. You need three kinds of people: someone ahead of you, someone beside you, someone rooting for you.

Find a mentor who makes you uncomfortable
Build your peer group of builders
Protect the rare ones who genuinely want you to win
🧭

Realm 05

Spiritually Competent

Nothing to do with religion. Everything to do with knowing who you are when no one is watching. What would you defend even if it cost you?

Define your core values — write them down
Act consistently whether seen or unseen
Know what matters beyond money and status
💰

Realm 06

Financially Competent

More money means more confidence. You walk different, talk different. But fix the first five realms — and the money follows naturally.

Track every dollar in and out
Build one income stream to $5k/mo before diversifying
People pay for competence — become undeniable

    Your Path

    The 6-Month Roadmap

    One realm per month. Compound the gains. By month six, you won't recognize yourself.

    ⚡

    Month 1

    Lock in your skill

    Choose one marketable skill. Block 2 hours per day. Ship something by week 4 — a project, a post, a client. Proof of work beats intentions.

    🔥

    Month 2

    Own your body

    Build the gym habit. Fix your sleep. Cut one bad food habit. You don't need a transformation — you need a baseline that signals discipline.

    🧠

    Month 3

    Harden your mind

    Do the hard thing first every morning. Journal for 5 minutes on what you avoided yesterday. Stop venting — start adapting. Take a cold shower.

    🤝

    Month 4

    Rebuild your circle

    Audit your 5 closest people. Reach out to one person ahead of you. Join one community aligned with your skill. Cut or limit energy vampires.

    🧭

    Month 5

    Find your compass

    Write your 5 core values. For one week, make every decision by them. Kill the habit or action that contradicts who you say you are.

    💰

    Month 6

    Get paid for who you became

    By now you have a skill, a body, a mindset, a network, and values. Package it. Charge for it. The money is the scoreboard — not the goal.
